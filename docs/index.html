Privacy Policy for Roominate

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Introduction

Roominate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Roominate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, preferences
  • Property Listings: Property details, photos, location, pricing, availability
  • Booking Information: Rental agreements, payment details, booking history
  • Communications: Messages between tenants and property owners
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID (for property owners), verification documents

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, approximate location (when permission granted)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app version

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication: OAuth data from Google/Facebook (if social login used)
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data from Lenco payment gateway
  • Verification Services: Identity verification results from third-party providers

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Facilitating property listings and rental bookings
  • Processing payments and financial transactions
  • Enabling communication between tenants and property owners
  • Providing location-based property search and recommendations

2.2 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyzing app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Personalizing content and property recommendations
  • Debugging and fixing technical issues
  • Conducting research and development

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Verifying user identity to prevent fraud
  • Detecting and preventing abuse, spam, or illegal activity
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Protecting the rights and safety of users

2.4 Communications

  • Sending transactional emails (booking confirmations, payment receipts)
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending service announcements and updates
  • Marketing communications (with your consent, opt-out available)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Public Profile: Name, photo, and bio visible to other users
  • Property Listings: Property details visible to potential tenants
  • Booking Details: Shared between tenant and property owner for confirmed bookings

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication hosting
  • Lenco: Payment processing
  • Resend: Email delivery service
  • Cloud Storage: Property photos and documents
  • Analytics Providers: App performance and usage analytics

3.3 Legal and Safety Reasons

  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Roominate, our users, or the public
  • In connection with business transfers (merger, acquisition, sale of assets)

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent.

4. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 90 days of account deletion (except as required by law)
  • Transaction Records: Financial records retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Legal Holds: Data preserved if subject to legal proceedings

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data in a portable format
  • Access your profile and account information through the app

5.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your profile information directly in the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

5.3 Deletion

  • Delete your account through the app settings
  • Request deletion of specific data (subject to legal obligations)

5.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Marketing Emails: Unsubscribe via link in emails or app settings
  • Location Services: Disable location permission in device settings
  • Push Notifications: Disable in device settings

5.5 Data Processing Objections

  • Object to automated decision-making or profiling
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where consent is the legal basis)

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure authentication, access controls, monitoring, and regular audits. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Roominate is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at privacy@roominate.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Review their privacy policies before providing information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notification, email to registered users, and a prominent notice in the app. Continued use of Roominate after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
